This is our story...

We are a married couple who had arrived in Canada with the idea of establishing a coffee shop in Ottawa, maybe Montreal too.

Our business idea is to offer you the fines selected organic Colombian coffee and homemade fruit infusion tea. The menu we offer is based in 3 different coffee classes, 5 home-made fruit infusion and herbal teas, and natural season beverages.

We want to show some of the history of Colombian coffee and the healthy advantages of herbal and fruit infusion tea.

We want to promote our products in the young adult to adult people, ages 18 to 40 years old as they are the principal buyers of our delicious coffee. For the people from 40 to 60 years old, it is more likely for us to have an impact with our 110% natural herbal infusion tea.
