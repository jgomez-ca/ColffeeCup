Our Eco-Policy

Our environmental policy starts by carefully select our coffee suppliers who must be certified as sustainable coffee producers by their local authority.

The coffee must have the “Triple Certified Coffee” which means the product is organic, shade-grown and Fair trade certified. The “Rainforest Alliance Certified Coffee” meets strict standards of environmental, social, and economic sustainability and that is the key image we want to promote in our shop.

By executing a strict plan for 3R cycle (reduce, reuse, recycle) in every step of the production process ColffeeCup wants to expand our commitment with the environment.
